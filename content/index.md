## Industries — Finance

### Overview

Singapore is Southeast Asia's leading financial hub and fintech centre, with 1,300+ operating fintech firms.

### Regulatory Authority

The Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) serves as both central bank and financial regulator.
- Website: [mas.gov.sg](https://www.mas.gov.sg)
- Fintech portal: [mas.gov.sg/development/fintech](https://www.mas.gov.sg/development/fintech)

### Key Legislation

| Act | Scope |
|-----|-------|
| Payment Services Act (PSA) | Digital payments, e-money, crypto payments |
| Securities and Futures Act (SFA) | Capital markets, digital token offerings |
| Financial Services and Markets Act (FSMA) | Crypto licensing for DTSPs (effective June 2025) |
| Financial Advisers Act (FAA) | Robo-advisors, financial advisory |

### Recent Initiatives

| Initiative | Description |
|------------|-------------|
| GFTN | Global Finance & Technology Network (launched Oct 2024) |
| BLOOM | Borderless, Liquid, Open, Online, Multi-currency — tokenised settlement |
| Stablecoin Framework | Single-currency stablecoin regulation, S$1M capital requirement |
| Project Guardian | Industry pilot for asset tokenisation |

## Industries — Green Energy

### Singapore Green Plan 2030

- Official portal: [greenplan.gov.sg](https://www.greenplan.gov.sg)
- Target: Net zero emissions by 2050

### Five Pillars

1. **City in Nature** — biodiversity, green spaces
2. **Sustainable Living** — reduce waste, green commutes
3. **Energy Reset** — solar, hydrogen, grid efficiency
4. **Green Economy** — green finance, sustainable businesses
5. **Resilient Future** — climate science, coastal protection

### Key Targets (by 2030)

| Target | Goal |
|--------|------|
| Solar energy | 5x current deployment |
| EV charging points | 60,000 |
| Public bus fleet | 50% electric |
| Diesel cars/taxis | No new registrations (from 2025) |
| Landfill waste | Reduce by 30% |
| New cars/taxis | 100% cleaner-energy models |

### Key Institutions

| Institution | Role | Website |
|-------------|------|---------|
| EMA | Energy market regulation | [ema.gov.sg](https://www.ema.gov.sg) |
| NEA | Environment protection | [nea.gov.sg](https://www.nea.gov.sg) |
| PUB | Water management | [pub.gov.sg](https://www.pub.gov.sg) |
| MSE | Sustainability policy | [mse.gov.sg](https://www.mse.gov.sg) |

### Focus Area: Low-Carbon Hydrogen

Singapore lacks land/sea space for large-scale solar or wind farms. Low-carbon hydrogen is being explored as the primary decarbonisation pathway.
